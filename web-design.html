Common questions.

How much does a website cost?

Simple landing page: $500-1,500. Business site (5-10 pages): $2,000-6,000. E-commerce: $4,000-15,000. Custom enterprise sites: $15,000+. Transparent pricing, no hidden fees. Free quote after a 30-min consultation.

How long does it take?

Landing page: 1-2 weeks. Business site: 3-5 weeks. E-commerce: 5-10 weeks. I give a firm timeline before starting and stick to it.

Do you use WordPress or custom code?

Depends on your needs. For content-heavy sites with frequent updates — WordPress makes sense. For performance-critical sites or unique designs — custom code. I recommend what fits YOUR situation, not what's easiest for me.

Can you design sites in multiple languages?

Yes. This very site is bilingual (Turkish/English) with automatic language detection and smooth switching. I can build multi-language sites for your global audience.

What about hosting and domain?

I help with both. Domain registration, hosting setup, DNS configuration, SSL certificates — all handled. You own everything, I just make it work.

Do you do redesigns of existing sites?

Yes. If your current site is slow, outdated, or not converting — I can modernize it. Usually involves a design refresh + technical improvements + SEO fixes.
