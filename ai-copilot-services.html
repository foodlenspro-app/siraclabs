Common questions.

Can't I just use ChatGPT or Claude directly?

You can for generic tasks. But ChatGPT doesn't know your codebase, customers, or company context. A custom copilot does — it's trained on your data, integrated into your tools, and optimized for your team's specific work. Much higher productivity gains.

How long does building a copilot take?

Simple focused copilot: 4-8 weeks. Full-featured integrated copilot with multiple data sources: 2-4 months. Enterprise-grade copilot with training, monitoring, and admin tools: 4-6 months.

What does a custom copilot cost?

Initial build: $15,000-80,000 depending on complexity. Ongoing costs: hosting + LLM API usage (typically $200-2,000/month for small teams). I provide detailed cost modeling.

Will my team actually use it?

Adoption is the make-or-break. I design copilots that feel natural to use — right inside tools people already use daily. I also help with team training and rollout. Most copilots I've built see 60-80% daily usage within 2 weeks.

Can it integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. I integrate with Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Notion, custom internal tools. The MCP protocol makes many integrations straightforward. Unusual integrations take extra work but are almost always possible.
