Common questions.

How much does a mobile app cost?

Simple MVP: $3,000-8,000. Medium complexity (with auth, payments, backend): $8,000-25,000. Complex enterprise apps: $25,000+. Store publishing always included. Free consultation to scope your specific app.

iOS or Android — which first?

For most global apps: Android first (bigger worldwide user base, easier approval). For premium/US-focused: iOS first. Best answer: Flutter means you don't choose — you get both from one codebase.

How long until I can launch?

MVP: 4-8 weeks. Full-featured app: 3-4 months. Enterprise apps: 4-6+ months. I provide weekly demo builds so you see progress and give feedback continuously.

Do you publish to the stores?

Yes. App Store and Google Play submission is part of my service. I handle signing, ASO metadata, screenshots, descriptions, categories, and review responses. You end up with a live app, not just code.

What about backend and hosting?

I can build it or integrate with yours. Firebase is my default (fast, reliable, affordable). Custom backends (Node.js, Python) for complex needs. All hosting setup included.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. Turkey-based but most of my work is global. English communication, async-friendly workflow, weekly video calls. Payment via Wise, Payoneer, or direct bank transfer.
